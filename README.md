# DesignFlowAI
AI-powered marketing platform prototype designed to help small businesses access professional marketing support through automation, AI-generated content, and intelligent customer service.
## Overview
DesignFlowAI was created as part of an AI and Business Innovation class project. The goal was to identify a real-world business problem and develop a technology-driven solution.
## Problem
Many small businesses struggle to create marketing content and manage customer engagement because hiring a full marketing team can be expensive.
## Solution
DesignFlowAI provides an AI-powered platform that helps businesses:
- Generate marketing content
- Access design assistance
- Improve customer engagement
- Receive automated support through an AI chatbot
